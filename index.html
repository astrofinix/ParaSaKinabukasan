“Education” is a word that has captivated the aspirations of Filipinos. The term has been associated with the pursuit of a better future, and has been deeply ingrained in the Filipino culture and values. Education is seen as a means to achieve success, to lift oneself out of poverty, and to contribute to society.

However, the question remains: has the education given to us fully made us change? Have we truly embraced the values and lessons taught to us in the pursuit of knowledge and growth?

These are important questions that require introspection and evaluation of our own beliefs and actions. Only then can we truly say that education has changed us for the better.
